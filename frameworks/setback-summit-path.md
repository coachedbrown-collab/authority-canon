# Setback → Summit → Path

## Overview
Internal diagnostic mapping sequence used to identify and resolve structural bottlenecks.

## The Sequence

### Setback
Identifies where the business is stuck. The current state of friction, drag, or founder dependency.

### Summit
Maps the structural ceiling. The theoretical limit of growth under the current architecture.

### Path
Installs the operational route out. The specific constraints to unlock and decision rights to assign to reach the next level of scale.
